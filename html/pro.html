The Real Price of Clean Air: EPA Ozone Regulation

Regulatory Economics · Causal Inference · Policy Analysis

Leverages the EPA's 0.071 ppm ozone attainment threshold in a regression discontinuity design to quantify the revenue impact on U.S. manufacturing firms. Finds that ozone regulations reduce nationwide manufacturing revenues by $277 million annually, a cost absent from the EPA's original regulatory impact analysis. Includes an interactive visualization of county-level ozone emissions nationwide.
